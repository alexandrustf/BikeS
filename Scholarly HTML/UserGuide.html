Client Guide

First, you will get on the home page, here you can read definitios about what is a bike.



Then, you can click on 'Servicii' and you will go to services page, where you can read and visualize what services we offer.



If you want more details, you can click on "Mai multe detalii" button, which it will lead you to another page which has informations and prices about specific services in our bikes service.



Then, if you decided, you can go to appointment page, by click on, "Rezervare". There you can watch the calendar and find a green day, you will select it from select point and click on "Vezi ore disponibile".

Monday through Friday, 8 to 16

The page will be reloaded and you will find that something new appeared. The available hours for your selected day. You must select an hour and complete your corect personal data in the formular.



If you want to add a video or an image with your bike with broken bike to make us understand what we should fix, don't hesitate to do it, but it is optional.



Press on "Programeaza" button. You will receive a response and if all goes well you will get the appointment answer on your specified email.



If there is something, you will get a message and you should complete the formular again, but be more careful to enter valid datas and fill all the inputs marked with *.



After 2 or 3 days you should check your email for the response for your appointment wrote by the administrator.

Admin Guide

First of all, you should login with your user and password(which was given to you by the developer).



The admin page has 3 main panels, where you can see, addapt, delete the appointments, stocks and orders. The first page is "Programare". Here you can see the programmed appointments, and accept with a price or decline with a reason the appointments which waits for a response. The response will be sent on their email.



If you click on 'Stocuri', you will get to stocks page. Here you can add or reduce the stock for a specific product according to your real life inventory. Also, there is a minimum stock of 20 items, if there are less 20 items of an item, an order(100 new items) to a provider will be made automatically.



If you click on 'Comenzi', you will get to orders page. Here you can make an order to a provider with a selected item and any quantity you wish. The order will arrive in one week from the moment you make the order.
